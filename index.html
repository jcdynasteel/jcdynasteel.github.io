Jose Carlos Gomez-Vazquez

Voice Actor. Engineer. Artist.

A man with a plan to deliver on all expectations!

Also known as JCDynasteel on other social media platforms, Jose Carlos is a bilingual English/Spanish Hispanic Voiceover Artist with experience in company narration and character work. Jose Carlos has been working as a software engineer for over 2 years. Expect the detailed attention he gives to his code to your amazing project!

He records in a treated home space on a Roswell Mini K87 and a Focusrite 2i2 (4th gen) using Reaper as his DAW. Jose Carlos has been coached by the legendary Joe Zieja, John Montoya, Natalie Shaw, Cristina Milizia, Erika Harlacher-Stone, Beau Stephenson, and David Rosenthal! He is currently a member of Joe Zieja's Pandora's Vox, an incredible team of voice actors under the tutelage of the above mentioned voiceover coaches.
